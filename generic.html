This is my story!

My journey starts at age 8 when I got my first videogame console, the PS1, which at that time had already been over 5 years on the market. It opened my eyes to the power of technology to create immersive stories and experiences.

I decided to pursue this passion as a career and studied Game and Simulator Development at UAI in Buenos Aires. Since then, I have been working in the industry, developing PC, Mobile and VR games and simulations using Unity and Unreal Engine. I have skills in scripting, game design, user interactions, level design, prototyping and team management.

Currently, I enjoy solving problems, facing challenges and learning new skills and I'm in search of an opportunity that allows me to contribute my passion, skills and creativity to an amazing team that wants to leave a mark in the gaming industry. Let's connect and explore how we can level up together!
